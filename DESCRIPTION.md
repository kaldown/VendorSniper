Never miss a limited-supply vendor restock again. Track the items you want across any vendor and get alerted when they are in stock.

## Features

- **Global watchlist** - Watch limited-supply items across any vendor, persists between sessions
- **Quantity targets** - Set how many you need; VendorSniper tracks progress toward your goal
- **Forever watch** - Mark items to buy on every visit with a configurable per-visit cap
- **Raid warning alerts** - Screen flash and looping sound when watched items are in stock (toggleable with `/vs alert`)
- **Buy All button** - One-click purchase of all watched items currently in stock
- **Purchase log** - Track what was bought, when, and from which vendor
- **Minimap button** - Left-click to toggle window

## Usage

1. Visit a vendor that sells limited-supply items
2. VendorSniper shows limited items with checkboxes
3. Check items you want to watch (shift-click for custom quantity)
4. Next time you visit any vendor with those items in stock, an alert fires
5. Click **Buy All** to purchase watched items, or buy them manually

**Tip:** Items are tracked globally - they can be bought from any vendor that sells them, not just the one you added them from.

## Slash Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/vs` | Toggle window |
| `/vs watch [itemlink]` | Add item to watchlist |
| `/vs clear` | Clear watchlist |
| `/vs status` | Show status and watched items |
| `/vs log` | Show recent purchase log |
| `/vs alert` | Toggle alert sound/overlay |

## Compatibility

Works on Classic Era (1.15.x) and Anniversary Edition (TBC 2.5.5).
